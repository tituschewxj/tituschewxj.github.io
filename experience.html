  1. May 2023

    Computing for Voluntary Welfare Organisations

    Computing for Voluntary Welfare Organisations (CVWO) is an NUS programme that builds and maintains quality IT systems for Voluntary Welfare Organisations (VWOs). Working in a team of 7, I maintained and improved the Case Management System (CMS). I ported the CMS backend from Ruby on Rails to Golang, resulting in significant performance improvements, as well as long term maintainability of the codebase. I also made bug fixes and enhancements for our partner VWOs, which streamlines their workflow, allowing them to spend their time and energy on helping their beneficiaries instead.

  2. January 2023

    Source Academy

    Source Academy is a platform used by NUS students to learn computational thinking in CS1101S, Programming Methodology I. Working as a pair, I designed and implemented Source Academy's Arcade2D module, which uses Phaser 3. This module provides students a way to create 2D games through applying what they have learnt.
